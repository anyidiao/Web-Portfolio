Scrabble with Stuff

Description

Like traditional Scrabble, players take turns placing letters (to form words) on a two- dimensional grid board, earning points for the words they place on the board. However, each player has a private view of her board and cannot view other players’ tiles.

In Scrabble with Stuff, in addition to letter tiles of the classic game, a player may purchase special tiles with the points she has earned so far. These special tiles are added to the player’s inventory of tiles, and may be played in a subsequent turn along with a word.
